Welcome, Foodies

"Chinese food tries to engage the mind, not just the palate. To provoke the intellect.."-Nicole Mones

Opened in 2012 by the Lin brothers, Lin's Kitchen serves modern Chinese food for the discerning NYC palate. It has been recognized for it's innovative take on Chinese cuisine by the NY Times, Eater, Gothamist, CNN, TimeOut and MOST importantly by their dear pocket sized abuelita.The brothers share the dream to bring world-wide recognition to the rich culinary history of Chinese through the fun and creative efforts of their craft.
